WHO IS DEVELOPER?
The team consists of roughly 20 members, sub-divided into three specialties: coders, designers, and business developers. In the summer of 2013, Thinc. initially started Chula Pop Bus app with the aim of solving a real-world problem through a project which has large-scale impact. After 20,000 downloads (as of mid-2014) and counting, Thinc. hopes it has.
