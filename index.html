WHAT IS CU POP BUS?
Pop Bus is a free shuttle bus service provided by Chulalongkorn University with six routes for its students, professors, and staff. The shuttle bus service operates on four routes, running from Mondays to Saturdays, 7AM – 7PM, with bus no. 1 and 2 exclusively on Saturdays.
